# BazaarCo Warranty & Authenticity Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

Under Nepal's E-Commerce Act 2081, warranty and guarantee conditions must be disclosed, and an intermediary platform must help ensure those terms are honored. This policy explains warranties and product authenticity on BazaarCo.

## 1. Seller & Manufacturer Warranty

- Warranty or guarantee applies **only when stated** on the product page.
- The warranty is provided by the seller or manufacturer, not by BazaarCo.
- Warranty duration, coverage, and claim conditions are shown on the listing.
- Products sold without a warranty are clearly labelled "No warranty".

## 2. BazaarCo's Role

BazaarCo does not manufacture products, but we help coordinate valid warranty claims between buyers and sellers and will enforce a seller's stated warranty commitment.

## 3. Product Authenticity

- Sellers are responsible for ensuring every product is genuine and legally sourced.
- Counterfeit, replica, or fake-branded goods are strictly prohibited.
- Sellers must hold the rights or authorization to sell branded products.

## 4. Reporting a Fake or Misrepresented Product

If you believe a product is counterfeit or not authentic:

1. Open the order in **My Orders** and file a complaint, or email support@bazaarconepal.com.
2. Provide photos, packaging images, and any serial/warranty information.
3. We investigate and, if confirmed, issue a refund and take seller action (listing removal, payout hold, or suspension).

## 5. Electronics & High-Value Goods

For electronics and similar goods, sellers must provide a warranty card and/or invoice where the manufacturer offers one. Keep these documents to support any future warranty claim.

## 6. How to Claim Warranty

1. Contact BazaarCo support with your order ID and a description of the fault.
2. We coordinate with the seller/manufacturer per the stated warranty terms.
3. Outcomes may include repair, replacement, or refund depending on the warranty.
