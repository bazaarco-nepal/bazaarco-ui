## 2. What "Intermediary Marketplace" Means

BazaarCo is an **intermediary platform** that connects independent sellers with buyers. Products listed on BazaarCo are owned and sold by third-party sellers. BazaarCo facilitates discovery, ordering, payment, and delivery coordination, and operates a grievance redressal mechanism.

This does not remove BazaarCo's responsibility to address complaints. Under the E-Commerce Act 2081, an e-commerce business cannot avoid handling a complaint merely because the goods were not manufactured, imported, or directly supplied by it.
