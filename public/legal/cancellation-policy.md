# BazaarCo Cancellation Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## 1. BUYER-INITIATED CANCELLATION

You may cancel an order at any time before it is dispatched (marked as "Shipped") at no cost.

### How to Cancel
- Go to your order page
- Click "Cancel Order"
- Confirm the cancellation
- You will receive a cancellation confirmation email

### Cancellation Timeline
- Request cancellation anytime before dispatch
- We process cancellations within 24 hours
- If the seller has already packed the order, we may not be able to cancel

### Conditions

Cancellations are free only if:
- The order has not been marked as dispatched
- No payment is currently in progress
- The order was not placed with a special promotion

If you cancel after the seller has dispatched the order, you'll need to use our Return & Refund Policy instead.

## 2. PREPAID ORDER REFUNDS (CANCELLATION)

If you cancel a prepaid order before dispatch:

### Refund Eligibility
- Full refund including VAT and all fees
- Refund issued within [X] business days
- Returned to original payment method

### Refund Timeline
- Credit/Debit card: 3-5 business days
- Mobile wallet (eSewa, Khalti): 2-3 business days
- Bank transfer: Up to 10 business days

### No Restocking Fees
- You pay nothing for cancellation
- No penalty or service charge
- We don't deduct any fees

## 3. CASH ON DELIVERY (COD) ORDERS

### Before Dispatch
- You may cancel anytime before dispatch
- No refund needed (payment not collected yet)
- Order is simply cancelled

### After Dispatch
- Cannot be cancelled
- Must accept delivery or refuse payment
- If you refuse, the order returns to seller
- You can then return the item under Return & Refund Policy

## 4. PLATFORM-INITIATED CANCELLATION

BazaarCo may cancel an order in these cases:

### Out of Stock
- Item becomes unavailable before dispatch
- We notify you immediately
- You receive a full refund with no penalty

### Fraudulent Activity
- We detect fraud or suspicious activity
- We cancel the order immediately
- Investigation may be required
- Refund issued if investigation finds no fraud

### Pricing Error
- Item was mispriced
- We may cancel the order or correct the price
- You'll be asked to confirm the correct price before proceeding
- If you don't confirm, order is cancelled and refunded

### Legal or Safety Issues
- Item violates Nepali law
- Item is dangerous or counterfeit
- Government order requires cancellation
- Full refund issued immediately

## 5. SELLER-INITIATED CANCELLATION

Sellers may cancel orders only in these cases:

### Legitimate Reasons
- Item is out of stock (rare, seller should update inventory)
- Fraudulent or suspicious order detected
- Customer requested cancellation (buyer asked seller to cancel)

### Seller Cannot Arbitrarily Cancel
- Cannot cancel because they want higher price
- Cannot cancel to avoid low ratings
- Cannot cancel after promising dispatch
- Cannot cancel to resell at higher price

If seller violates this:
- We force a refund
- Seller receives a strike
- Account may be suspended or terminated

## 6. PARTIAL CANCELLATION

If you ordered multiple items in one order:

You may:
- Cancel the entire order
- Request cancellation of specific items only

### Process
- Contact us with item details
- We arrange partial cancellation/refund
- Other items may continue to dispatch

If seller has already dispatched all items, you must use Return & Refund Policy for items you don't want.

## 7. ONCE DISPATCHED, YOU MUST RETURN

After the order is marked as "Shipped" or "Out for Delivery":

**You cannot cancel.**

Instead, you can:
- Accept delivery and return the item (Return & Refund Policy)
- Refuse delivery (don't pay for COD, refuse package)
- Use Return & Refund Policy for refund

If item is refused at delivery:
- It returns to seller
- Seller or BazaarCo will initiate refund
- You'll be asked to authorize return processing
- Refund issued after item is verified

## 8. DISPUTES ABOUT CANCELLATION

If we deny your cancellation request:

You can:
- Contact our support team (support@bazaarconepal.com)
- Request explanation within 24 hours
- Provide evidence or context
- Appeal the decision

If unresolved, file a grievance (15-day resolution, per E-Commerce Act 2081).
