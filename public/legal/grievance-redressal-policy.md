# BazaarCo Grievance Redressal Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

Under Nepal's E-Commerce Act 2081, we are required to maintain a grievance redressal system with guaranteed timelines. This policy explains how to file complaints and what to expect.

## 1. WHAT IS A GRIEVANCE?

### A grievance is a formal complaint about

- Product quality (defective, damaged, misdescribed)
- Seller conduct (fraud, non-delivery, poor service)
- Delivery issues (delay, damage in transit, wrong address)
- Payment problems (unauthorized charges, failed refunds)
- Account issues (unauthorized access, data breach)
- Service quality (poor customer support)
- Return/refund disputes (refund not processed)
- Scam or fraudulent activity
- Violation of BazaarCo policies
- Any other issue affecting your rights

### A grievance is NOT

- A product review (use reviews for that)
- A suggestion or feedback (email us instead)
- A pricing complaint (prices are set by sellers)
- A general inquiry (email support instead)

## 4. GRIEVANCE INVESTIGATION PROCESS

### Timeline: GUARANTEED 15 DAYS (E-Commerce Act 2081)

**Days 1-2: Acknowledgment**
- We confirm receipt within 24 hours
- Assign case to grievance officer
- Contact seller for their version
- Request evidence from both sides

**Days 3-7: Investigation**
- Gather evidence and documentation
- Interview relevant parties
- Review BazaarCo's logs
- Analyze applicable policy

**Days 8-14: Decision**
- Evaluate evidence
- Determine facts
- Apply policy/law
- Draft decision

**Day 15: Resolution**
- Notify you of decision
- Explain reasoning
- If refund approved, issue immediately
- If seller action needed, enforce it

**Days 16+: If Extended**
- For complex cases needing more time
- You will be notified within 15 days
- Extended deadline provided (max 30 days)
- Regular status updates

## 5. TYPES OF RESOLUTIONS

### Full Refund
- For defective, misdescribed, or undelivered items
- Including taxes and delivery fees
- Issued within 5 business days

### Partial Refund
- For items with minor issues
- Refund calculated fairly
- Deduction explained in writing

### Replacement
- New item shipped at seller's expense
- Free return shipping provided
- Must arrive within [X] days

### Store Credit
- Alternative to refund
- Use for any purchase on BazaarCo
- Non-refundable but transferable

### Seller Action
- Seller ordered to fulfill commitment
- (E.g., fix issue, send missing item)
- Enforced by BazaarCo
- Deadline given

### Dismissal
- Complaint found to be baseless
- Evidence supported seller's position
- You are notified why

## 6. STANDARDS OF EVIDENCE

We determine facts based on:

**Strong Evidence:**
- Photos/videos of the issue
- Order history and proof of payment
- Tracking information
- Communication records
- Seller's own admissions

**Moderate Evidence:**
- Detailed written account
- Witness statements
- Patterns (seller has done this before)
- BazaarCo's own investigation findings

**Limited Evidence:**
- Vague descriptions
- "Someone told me"
- Accusations without support
- Conflicting stories

**Balance of Probabilities:**
- We decide based on "more likely than not"
- Not "beyond reasonable doubt" (that's criminal)
- We favor factual, documented evidence

## 7. SELLER OBLIGATIONS

### During a Grievance

Sellers must:
- Respond within 48 hours
- Provide honest information
- Not contact customer outside formal channels
- Comply with our investigation
- Not retaliate or harass the customer

### Sellers cannot
- Refuse to cooperate
- Delete evidence
- Ignore communication
- Cancel the order to avoid resolution
- Blackmail or threaten

### Failure to Comply
- Forced compliance by BazaarCo
- Strike against seller account
- Increased liability for damages
- Possible account suspension

## 8. YOUR RIGHTS DURING GRIEVANCE

You have the right to:

- File without fear of retaliation
- Privacy (your information kept confidential)
- Have our decision explained
- Request a review if new evidence emerges
- Appeal if you disagree
- Escalate to government if unsatisfied
- Receive compensation if BazaarCo is at fault

## 9. APPEALS & ESCALATION

### If You Disagree with Our Decision

Within 14 days of decision, you can:
1. Request review (new evidence only)
2. Appeal to Grievance Manager (management review)
3. Request escalation to DoCSCP
4. File with government bodies
5. Pursue legal action (court)

### Internal Review Process
- You must provide new evidence

- Decision within 7 days
