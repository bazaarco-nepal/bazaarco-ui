## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device (computer, phone, tablet). They contain information about your browsing activity.

### Types of Cookies

- **First-party cookies:** Set by BazaarCo
- **Third-party cookies:** Set by other services (analytics, ads)
- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Stay on your device for months or years
- **HTTP-only cookies:** Cannot be accessed by JavaScript (more secure)

## 2. WHY WE USE COOKIES

### Essential Cookies (Always On - No Consent Required)

We use cookies for:
- Keeping you logged in
- Maintaining your shopping cart
- Security and fraud prevention
- Remembering your preferences (language, currency)
- Protecting against CSRF attacks
- Performing analytics to understand how you use our site
- Technical performance monitoring

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate business interest and contract fulfillment.
These cookies are technically necessary and cannot be turned off.

### Optional Cookies (Require Your Consent)

**Non-Essential Cookies:**
- Google Analytics (behavior tracking)
- Facebook Pixel (retargeting)
- Personalization and recommendations
- Marketing and advertising
- A/B testing
- User preference tracking

These are **NOT necessary** for the site to function. We only load these after you explicitly consent.

## 3. COOKIE MANAGEMENT ON FIRST VISIT

### Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit BazaarCo:
1. A banner appears at the bottom of the page
2. You can:
   - Click "Accept All" (load all cookies)
   - Click "Reject Non-Essential" (load only essential)
   - Click "Manage Preferences" (choose granularly)

### What Happens
- Your choice is remembered for [365] days
- You can change your preference anytime
- No cookies load before you decide (except minimal technical cookies)

### Legal Basis
Nepal's Privacy Act 2075 requires explicit, non-pre-ticked consent for non-essential tracking.

### Our Commitment
- We never pre-tick consent boxes
- Essential cookies work even if you reject all others
- You can browse our site with zero tracking cookies if you wish

## 4. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

### Change Preferences Anytime

**Method 1: Browser Settings**
- Go to your browser settings
- Find "Cookies" or "Privacy"
- Block or allow specific websites
- Delete existing cookies

**Method 2: BazaarCo Account**
- Login to your account
- Go to Settings → Privacy & Cookies
- Toggle individual cookie categories on/off
- Save preferences

**Method 3: Cookie Banner**
- Click the cookie icon (bottom right, or look for banner link)
- Re-open the consent banner
- Update your choices

## 5. ESSENTIAL COOKIES WE USE

These cookies are always active. You cannot opt out (they're technically necessary).

### Session Cookies
- `session_id`: Keeps you logged in
- `csrf_token`: Prevents security attacks
- `cart_data`: Remembers your shopping cart
- `user_preferences`: Language, currency, theme

### Security Cookies
- `auth_token`: Verifies your identity
- `ip_verify`: Detects suspicious logins
- `rate_limit`: Prevents brute-force attacks

### Performance Cookies
- `page_timing`: Tracks page load speed
- `error_logs`: Captures JavaScript errors
- `perf_metrics`: Monitors server response time

**Retention:** Session cookies deleted when you close the browser; security cookies kept for [30] days.

## 7. DO NOT TRACK (DNT)

### If You Enable DNT

- Your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal
- We respect this signal for non-essential cookies
- Essential cookies still load (necessary for site function)

### How to Enable DNT

- **Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy → Check "Send websites a 'Do Not Track' signal"
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy → Turn on "Do Not Track"
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Check "Ask websites not to track me"
- **Internet Explorer:** Safety → Tracking Protection
