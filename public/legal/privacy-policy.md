# BazaarCo Privacy Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## 1. WHAT WE COLLECT

### Account Data
- Name, email address, phone number
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- Profile picture

### Delivery & Address Information
- Delivery addresses
- Billing addresses
- Contact phone numbers

### Order & Transaction Data
- Order history and details
- Purchase amounts and dates
- Payment method (tokenized, never raw card data)
- Refund and return information

### Device & Usage Data
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type and version
- IP address
- Pages visited and interaction patterns
- Search queries
- Click-through data

### Seller-Specific Data (KYC)
- Citizenship/passport number
- Company registration documents
- PAN/VAT number
- Bank account details
- Business address
- Beneficial owner information

## 2. WHY WE COLLECT IT

We collect personal data to:

- Create and manage your account
- Process and deliver your orders
- Process payments and refunds
- Verify your identity and prevent fraud
- Meet legal and tax obligations
- Improve our platform and services
- Send order updates and important notices
- Respond to customer support requests
- Comply with government requirements (DoCSCP, IRD, etc.)
- Send marketing communications (only with your consent)

## 3. LAWFUL BASIS & CONSENT

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

- Your consent (provided at signup)
- Contract fulfillment (to process your orders)
- Legal obligation (tax, DoCSCP, E-Commerce Act 2081)
- Fraud prevention and security
- Legitimate business interests

Marketing consent is separate and optional. You can withdraw marketing consent anytime without affecting your account.

We comply with Nepal's Privacy Act 2075 and E-Commerce Act 2081.

## 5. WHO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH

### Sellers
- Only the information necessary to fulfill your order (name, delivery address, contact phone)
- Not your payment card information or password

### Logistics & Delivery Partners
- Delivery address and phone number
- Order tracking information
- Name for delivery verification

### Payment Gateways
- NRB-licensed payment providers (eSewa, Khalti, Fonepay, ConnectIPS)
- We do not share raw card data; payment gateways handle it directly

### Government & Legal Authorities
- When legally required by court order, warrant, or law enforcement
- Tax authorities (IRD) for compliance
- DoCSCP for platform oversight

### Service Providers
- Email/SMS providers for order notifications
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Support platform providers

**We do NOT sell your data to third parties for marketing.**

## 6. YOUR RIGHTS

Under Nepal's Privacy Act 2075, you have the right to:

- **Access:** View all personal data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Export:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Revoke Consent:** Withdraw marketing consent anytime
- **Object:** Object to certain processing activities
- **Complaint:** File a complaint with the Privacy Officer

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bazaarconepal.com, WhatsApp/phone +977 9700053075, or visit your account settings.

## 7. SECURITY

We protect your data using:

- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
- Secure password hashing (Argon2id)
- Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Intrusion detection systems
- Incident response procedures
- Employee data protection training

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities within 30 days, as required by law.
