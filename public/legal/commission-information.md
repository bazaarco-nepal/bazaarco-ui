BazaarCo charges a commission on all sales made through the platform. This document outlines our commission policy and how it works.

## 3. How Commission is Calculated

1. Commission is calculated on the **selling price** (excluding any discounts applied by the seller)
2. Commission is **automatically deducted** from the seller's payout after a successful transaction
3. Tax (VAT) is calculated on the product price **before** commission deduction

## 4. Payment & Settlement

- Seller payouts are processed **within 3-5 business days** after order delivery
- Commission is deducted at the time of payout
- Detailed commission breakdowns are available in your seller dashboard

## 5. Special Cases

- **Cancelled Orders:** No commission is charged
- **Refunded Orders:** Commission is refunded to the seller within 3-5 business days
- **Return/Refund:** Commission on returned items is credited back to the seller
