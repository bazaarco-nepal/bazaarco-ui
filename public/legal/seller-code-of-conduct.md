# BazaarCo Seller Code of Conduct

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

Every seller on BazaarCo agrees to this Code of Conduct. It sets the practical, non-negotiable standards for selling on a fair, trustworthy Nepali marketplace. It works alongside the [Seller Policy](/legal/seller-policy) and the binding [Seller Agreement](/legal/seller-agreement).

## 1. Honest Products & Listings

- Sell only genuine, legal, and safely sourced products.
- No counterfeit, fake-branded, or replica goods.
- No misleading listings — titles, images, videos, and descriptions must match the real product.
- Follow the [Product Listing Rules](/legal/product-listing-rules) for required disclosures.

## 2. Keep Transactions on BazaarCo

- Do not redirect buyers to Instagram, WhatsApp, or any channel to complete a sale off-platform.
- Do not solicit offline payments or "deal poaching" to avoid commission.
- All orders that originate on BazaarCo must be completed on BazaarCo. BazaarCo is not liable for transactions taken outside the platform, and off-platform poaching may lead to suspension.

## 3. Integrity & Fair Dealing

- No fraud, bribery, fake orders, or manipulation of the platform.
- No fake reviews and no posing as a customer to review your own products (see [Reviews & Community Guidelines](/legal/reviews-and-guidelines)).
- Honour your stated prices, warranty, and return commitments.

## 4. Respectful Conduct

- Treat buyers, support staff, and other sellers with respect.
- No harassment, threats, intimidation, or abusive language.

## 5. Lawful & Ethical Sourcing

- Comply with Nepali law, including the [Prohibited & Restricted Products Policy](/legal/prohibited-products-policy).
- No child labour and no forced or bonded labour anywhere in your sourcing.
- Respect others' rights under the [Intellectual Property Policy](/legal/intellectual-property-policy).

## 6. Enforcement

Breaching this Code may result in listing removal, payout holds, account suspension, or permanent termination, depending on severity and history. Serious violations (counterfeit goods, fraud, off-platform poaching) may lead to immediate suspension.
