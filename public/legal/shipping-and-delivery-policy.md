# BazaarCo Shipping & Delivery Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## 1. DELIVERY INFORMATION AT CHECKOUT

Before you purchase, you will see:

- Estimated delivery date (or date range)
- Delivery charge (if any)
- Available delivery methods
- Seller location and dispatch timeline

All information is binding. The price shown includes delivery cost. **No surprise charges will be added.**

## 2. DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES

Each seller sets their own delivery timeframe:

### Standard Delivery
- [3-7] business days (typical)
- May vary by location (Kathmandu vs. remote areas)
- Depends on seller's ability to dispatch quickly

### Express Delivery
- [1-2] business days
- Higher cost (shown at checkout)
- Limited to certain sellers and areas

### Weekend & Holiday Delays
- Saturdays and Sundays: No delivery (standard)
- National holidays: No delivery
- If delivery date falls on holiday, it moves to next business day

### Delays

If a seller cannot deliver by the promised date:
- They must notify you within 24 hours
- New estimated date will be provided
- Delay beyond [X] days allows order cancellation/refund

## 3. DELIVERY COVERAGE & AREAS

### Available Delivery Areas
- All of Nepal's major cities and towns
- Remote areas: [List specific excluded areas or high-charge areas]
- Some sellers may not deliver to all areas (shown at checkout)

### Unavailable for Delivery
If your area is not served:
- You cannot place an order for delivery there
- COD may not be available
- You will be notified before purchase

## 4. DELIVERY RESPONSIBILITY & RISK

### Risk of Loss
- **Before delivery:** BazaarCo and seller are responsible for the item
- **At delivery:** Risk passes to you (you own the item upon delivery)
- **Damage in transit:** BazaarCo/logistics company is liable

### Delivery Confirmation
- Courier will call you before delivery
- You must accept the package at the address provided
- Signature or OTP confirmation required
- Receipt will be given

### Refused Delivery
- If you refuse to accept the package, it returns to seller
- Seller initiates return/refund
- You may incur return shipping costs

## 5. CHECKING YOUR DELIVERY

### Upon Receipt
Check the package immediately:
- Verify package is not damaged
- Check contents match order
- Verify item condition

### Reporting Damage
- Report visible damage within 24 hours
- Take photos of packaging and damage
- Contact us immediately at support@bazaarconepal.com
- We will investigate and process a refund or replacement

### Late Reporting
- If you report damage after 24 hours, it's harder to prove
- Logistics company may deny the claim
- We will still try to help, but success is not guaranteed

## 6. TRACKING

### Order Tracking
- You receive tracking number via email
- Track your order on our website anytime
- Real-time updates on shipment status
- Estimated delivery date will be shown

### Tracking Statuses
- **Order Confirmed:** Seller received the order
- **Packed:** Item is being prepared
- **Dispatched:** Handed to courier
- **In Transit:** On the way to you
- **Out for Delivery:** Arriving today
- **Delivered:** Successfully delivered
- **Pending Payment:** Awaiting COD payment
- **Failed Delivery:** Courier could not deliver
- **Returned:** Item returned to seller

## 7. FAILED DELIVERY

### If the Courier Cannot Deliver

**Reasons:**
- Address is incorrect or incomplete
- You are not available
- Address is unreachable
- Security restrictions

**What Happens:**
- Courier will attempt 2-3 times
- You will be contacted to reschedule
- After [3] failed attempts, item is returned
- Refund is initiated

**To Prevent Failure:**
- Provide a correct, complete address
- Be available on delivery day
- Provide clear directions to your location
- Respond to courier's calls

## 8. DELIVERY TO THIRD PARTIES

You may authorize someone else to receive delivery:

### Process
- Inform us who will receive it
- Provide their name and phone number
- They must have valid ID for pickup
- They will sign the receipt

### Liability
- Once received by the third party, it's considered delivered
- BazaarCo is not liable for issues after delivery to the third party
- You are responsible for the third party's actions

## 9. ADDRESS VERIFICATION

You are responsible for providing:

- Complete address
- Correct phone number (courier will call)
- Clear directions (if hard to find)
- Contact person name

If address is incomplete or incorrect:

- Courier may fail to deliver
- Item will be returned
- Refund will be delayed
- You may be charged return shipping

## 10. DELIVERY CHARGES

### Delivery Charges
- Clearly shown before checkout
- Varies by seller and location
- Included in total price (no hidden fees)
- Free delivery may be offered on certain orders
- Refunded if you cancel before dispatch

## 11. GOVERNING LAW & LIABILITY

This policy complies with Nepal's Consumer Protection Act 2075 and E-Commerce Act 2081.

### Liability Limits
- **Courier liability:** Limited by their terms
- **BazaarCo liability:** We will investigate and help resolve issues
- **Seller liability:** For items that are defective or misdescribed
- **Your liability:** For damage caused by you or incorrect address
