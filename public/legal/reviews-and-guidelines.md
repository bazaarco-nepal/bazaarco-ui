# BazaarCo Reviews & Community Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## 1. REVIEW ELIGIBILITY

### Who Can Review?

Only customers who purchased and received an item can review it.

**Requirements:**
- Order must be delivered (marked as "Delivered")
- Item must have been purchased [X] days to [X] days ago
- Account must be [X] days old
- Account must not be suspended or marked as fraudulent

### No Fake Reviews Allowed

You cannot:
- Write reviews of items you didn't purchase
- Pay others to write reviews for you
- Impersonate other customers
- Use multiple accounts to review the same item
- Copy reviews from other websites

### Criminal Penalties

Writing or paying for fake reviews is a **CRIMINAL OFFENSE** under E-Commerce Act 2081 §23:
- **Fine:** NPR 50,000–500,000
- **Imprisonment:** 6 months–3 years

BazaarCo reports confirmed fake reviewers to law enforcement.

## 2. WHAT MAKES A GOOD REVIEW

We encourage honest, helpful reviews that include:

✓ Your real experience with the product
✓ Product quality, durability, and value
✓ Accuracy of product description
✓ Delivery experience
✓ Seller's responsiveness
✓ Rating (1-5 stars)
✓ Photos or videos of the product
✓ Specific examples (e.g., "batteries lasted 6 months")

Helpful reviews explain:
- What you liked and didn't like
- Who should (or shouldn't) buy this product
- Comparison to similar products
- Long-term durability
- Value for money

A good review is 50+ words and includes specific details.

## 3. WHAT YOU MUST NOT POST

### Prohibited Content

We will remove reviews that:

**Defamatory or False:**
- Make false claims about the product
- Claim the seller is dishonest without evidence
- Contain false accusations
- Damage the seller's reputation unfairly

**Abusive or Hateful:**
- Use profanity or slurs
- Contain racist, sexist, or discriminatory language
- Threaten or harass the seller
- Bully other reviewers

**Off-Topic:**
- Discuss politics or social issues unrelated to the product
- Advertise other products or services
- Share complaints about BazaarCo (use customer support instead)
- Spam or marketing content
- Unrelated personal stories

**Illegal Content:**
- Promote illegal activity
- Contain pirated or copyrighted content
- Share confidential information
- Contain malware or phishing links

**PII (Personal Information):**
- Your full address or contact info
- Payment card numbers
- Seller's personal phone number or home address
- Other customer's private information

## 4. RATINGS EXPLAINED

**5 Stars (Excellent):**
- Product matches description perfectly
- High quality and durability
- Delivery was on time
- Would definitely buy again

**4 Stars (Good):**
- Product is good but has minor issues
- Generally matches description
- Minor delivery delays
- Worth the price

**3 Stars (Average):**
- Product is okay but could be better
- Some issues or inconsistencies
- Acceptable quality
- Fair price

**2 Stars (Poor):**
- Product has significant issues
- Notable gaps between description and reality
- Multiple quality problems
- Poor value for price

**1 Star (Very Poor):**
- Product is broken, defective, or unusable
- Drastically different from description
- Completely unacceptable quality
- Major disappointment

## 5. VERIFIED PURCHASE BADGE

### Only Verified Purchases Show a Badge

The "Verified Purchase" badge means:
- This person actually bought this item on BazaarCo
- The order has been delivered
- Their review is authentic

**Badge appears when:**
✓ Review posted by verified customer
✓ Order status is "Delivered"
✓ Within [X] days of delivery

## 6. RESPONDING TO REVIEWS

### Sellers Can Respond

Sellers are allowed to:
- Politely respond to customer reviews
- Offer to resolve issues
- Correct factual errors in reviews
- Thank customers for positive feedback

### Sellers CANNOT

- Demand removal of negative reviews
- Harass or threaten reviewers
- Bribe reviewers
- Impersonate customers in responses
- Post off-topic content

### Customers Can Reply

Customers can reply to seller responses to clarify or provide more information.

## 7. MODERATION & ENFORCEMENT

### How We Monitor

- Automated system flags suspicious reviews
- Manual review team checks removed reviews
- AI detects patterns (multiple accounts, fake reviews)
- User reports

### Our Actions

**Minor Violations:**
- Review is hidden (not deleted)
- Reviewer is notified
- 24-hour correction window
- Review restored if corrected

**Major Violations:**
- Review is removed
- Reviewer account warning issued
- Repeated violations = account suspension

**Severe/Criminal Violations:**
- Account suspended immediately
- Review removed
- Referred to law enforcement
- Cooperation with investigation

## 8. REVIEW MANIPULATION

### We Detect & Punish

**Multiple Accounts:**
- Using fake accounts to leave reviews
- Leaving identical reviews from different accounts
- **Result:** All reviews removed, accounts banned

**Competitor Attacks:**
- Leaving fake negative reviews on competitors' products
- Impersonating dissatisfied customers
- **Result:** Removal, account suspension, possible legal action

**Bribed Reviews:**
- Paying customers for positive reviews
- Offering discounts in exchange for 5-star reviews
- **Result:** Removal, account suspension, seller strike

**Rating Manipulation:**
- Asking customers to rate high artificially
- Promising benefits for positive ratings
- **Result:** Seller consequences (strikes, suspension)

## 9. SELLER RESPONSIBILITIES

Sellers must:

- Not pay for or solicit fake reviews
- Not leave reviews of their own products (conflict of interest)
- Not harass or threaten reviewers
- Respond professionally to reviews
- Address legitimate complaints
- Not take revenge on negative reviewers
- Not ask customers to remove negative reviews

**Violating these results in:**
- Strike against account
- Suspension
- Account termination
- Possible legal action

## 10. SELLER STRIKES SYSTEM

Sellers receive strikes for:

**1st Strike:**
- Refusing legitimate returns
- Harassing reviewers
- Manipulating reviews
- **Result:** Warning

**2nd Strike:**
- Account restricted (cannot list new products for 7 days)
- All listings reviewed
- Seller contacted

**3rd Strike:**
- Account suspended (14 days)
- Required training/review
- Risk of termination

**4th Strike:**
- Permanent account termination
- Removal from platform

## 11. APPEALS

### Believe Your Review Was Wrongly Removed?

**Process:**
1. Email support@bazaarconepal.com with your review details
2. Provide evidence why it should be restored
3. We review within 7 days
4. You receive a decision with explanation

**If Still Unhappy:**
- You can escalate to our Grievance Officer
- Final appeal within 30 days
- Decision is final

## 12. GOVERNING LAW & CRIMINAL PENALTIES

This policy complies with:

- E-Commerce Act 2081 (especially §23 on fake reviews)
- Consumer Protection Act 2075
- Penal Code 2074 (defamation, false statements)

### Criminal Penalties for Violations

**Fake Reviews:**
- NPR 50,000–500,000 fine
- 6 months–3 years imprisonment

**Defamation:**
- Up to 3 years imprisonment
- Civil liability for damages

**Harassment:**
- Up to 1 year imprisonment
- Restraining orders

BazaarCo cooperates fully with law enforcement on these investigations.
