# BazaarCo Product Listing Rules

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

Under Section 6 of Nepal's E-Commerce Act 2081, several product-level details must be displayed to buyers. These rules tell sellers exactly what every listing must include.

## 1. Required Listing Fields

| Product field                 | Requirement                                   |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Product name                  | Clear, accurate name                          |
| Category                      | Correct BazaarCo category                     |
| Brand / trademark             | Where applicable                              |
| Image / video                 | Must match the real product                   |
| Weight / material / size      | Where applicable                              |
| Final price incl. tax         | Required                                      |
| Delivery charges              | Required                                      |
| Warranty / guarantee          | If applicable                                 |
| Manufacturer / seller details | Required where applicable                     |
| Country of origin             | Required for imported goods                   |
| Manufacture / expiry date     | Required for food, cosmetics, health products |
| Returnable / non-returnable   | Required                                      |
| Cancellation availability     | Required                                      |
| Usage precautions             | For electronics, beauty, and health products  |

## 2. Accurate Media

- Images and videos must show the actual product the buyer will receive.
- No stock-photo misrepresentation, misleading edits, or borrowed media you do not have rights to.
- See our [Intellectual Property Policy](/legal/intellectual-property-policy) for image and content rights.

## 3. Honest Pricing

- Display the final price **including tax**.
- Disclose any additional delivery charges clearly.
- No hidden fees added after checkout.

## 4. Returns & Cancellation Disclosure

Every listing must state whether the item is returnable or non-returnable and whether cancellation is available before dispatch, consistent with the [Return & Refund Policy](/legal/return-and-refund-policy).

## 5. Restricted Categories

Some products require a licence or special conditions and may not be listed unless those are met. See the [Prohibited & Restricted Products Policy](/legal/prohibited-products-policy).

## 6. Enforcement

Listings that breach these rules may be edited, hidden, or removed. Repeated violations may lead to payout holds or seller suspension under the [Seller Policy](/legal/seller-policy).
