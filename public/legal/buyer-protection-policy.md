# BazaarCo Buyer Protection Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

BazaarCo is committed to making every purchase safe. This policy explains how we protect buyers when something goes wrong with an order.

## 1. What Is Covered

Buyer Protection applies to eligible orders placed and paid through BazaarCo. You are protected in the following situations:

| Situation                  | What you get                                 |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Product not received       | Full refund if the order is not delivered    |
| Wrong item delivered       | Return, replacement, or full refund          |
| Defective or damaged item  | Return, replacement, or full refund          |
| Item not as described      | Return and refund (item not used or damaged) |
| Counterfeit / fake product | Investigation, refund, and seller action     |
| Excessive seller delay     | Cancellation and refund                      |

## 2. How Payment Is Protected

For prepaid orders, funds are held and settled to the seller based on order completion. If a valid dispute is raised within the protection window, eligible amounts are refunded to you.

## 3. How to Make a Claim

1. Go to **My Orders** and open the affected order.
2. Click **File Complaint / Raise Dispute**.
3. Describe the issue and attach photos or video evidence.
4. Submit. You receive a ticket number and acknowledgment within 24 hours.

You may also email support@bazaarconepal.com or message +977 9700053075.

## 4. Claim Window

- Raise a claim **within 7 days** of delivery for most items.
- For non-delivery, raise a claim once the expected delivery date has passed.
- Claims are resolved under our [Grievance Redressal Policy](/legal/grievance-redressal-policy) within 15 days.

## 5. What Is Not Covered

- Buyer's remorse on non-returnable items (see [Return & Refund Policy](/legal/return-and-refund-policy)).
- Damage caused by the buyer after delivery.
- Items where the seal was broken on sealed goods, perishables, or hygiene items, except where defective.
- Claims raised after the protection window without valid reason.

## 6. Refund Method & Timeline

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method, wallet, or bank transfer, generally within **3–7 working days** of approval, including applicable taxes where the law requires.
