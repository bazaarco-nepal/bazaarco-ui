## 1. ELIGIBILITY & ACCOUNT VERIFICATION

You must meet all the following requirements to become a BazaarCo seller:

- Be at least 18 years of age
- Be a legal resident or registered business entity in Nepal
- Provide accurate and complete Know-Your-Customer (KYC) information
- Pass identity and background verification
- Not have a history of fraudulent activity or policy violations
- Not be listed on any government watchlists or banned persons list

### Identity Verification (KYC)

**Individuals must provide:**
- Full legal name
- Citizenship number or passport number
- Date of birth
- Permanent address
- PAN/VAT number
- Bank account details (for settlement)
- Mobile phone (verified)

**Businesses must provide:**
- Company registration certificate
- PAN/VAT certificate
- Beneficial owner information
- Bank account details (company account)
- Office address
- Business license (if applicable)

We verify all KYC information with government records. You must update your KYC information within 48 hours of any change.

## 2. YOUR LISTINGS

- No hidden charges or fees
- Compliant with all applicable laws

You are responsible for the accuracy of all information in your listings.
