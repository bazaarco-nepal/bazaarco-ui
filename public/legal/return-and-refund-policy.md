# BazaarCo Return & Refund Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## 1. YOUR RIGHT TO RETURN

Under Nepal's E-Commerce Act 2081, you have a guaranteed right to return goods that:

- Do not match the description, specification, or quality advertised
- Are defective or of poor quality
- Are counterfeit or not authentic
- Are not as described or promised

These returns are allowed regardless of how much time has passed.

Additionally, we offer a general return window of [7] days from delivery for any reason, provided goods are unused and undamaged.

## 2. RETURN WINDOW

### General Returns
- [7] days from delivery date
- Goods must be unused and in original condition
- Original packaging preferred but not required
- Applies to most products

### Defective, Counterfeit, or Misdescribed Goods
- No time limit
- We will accept the return at any time
- Full refund guaranteed

### Non-Returnable Items (Flagged at Checkout)
- Opened cosmetics or personal hygiene products
- Unsealed innerwear or undergarments
- Perishable items (food, fresh produce, flowers)
- Custom-made or personalized goods
- Items damaged by you (buyer fault)
- Items missing original packaging or seals

All non-returnable categories are clearly marked on the product page and again at checkout.

## 3. HOW TO RETURN

### Step 1: Request Return
- Go to your order page
- Select "Return" next to the item
- Select the reason for return
- Submit the return request

### Step 2: Receive Return Authorization
- We will email you a return authorization number
- Instructions for return will be provided (pickup or drop-off)
- Keep this number for tracking

### Step 3: Ship the Item Back
- Pack the item securely
- Use the shipping method we provide
- Include the return authorization number
- Keep the tracking number

### Step 4: Verification & Refund
- We inspect the returned item
- We confirm it matches return conditions
- We process your refund

Return Shipping:
- Free return shipping for defective/misdescribed items
- Prepaid return labels will be provided
- For general returns, standard return shipping rates apply

## 4. REFUNDS

### Refund Amount

You will receive a **FULL REFUND** including:
- Product price
- VAT (13%)
- Any delivery fees you paid
- Any applicable service fees

You will NOT receive a refund for:
- Shipping for general returns (only for defective items)
- Cost of replacement items you purchased
- Restocking fees (we don't charge these)

### Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method:
- **Credit/Debit card:** 3-5 business days
- **eSewa/Khalti/mobile money:** 2-3 business days
- **Cash on Delivery:** Store credit or bank transfer (up to 10 business days)
- You may choose store credit instead for faster processing

### Refund Timeline

- We verify the returned item within [5] business days
- Once verified, we process the refund within [X] business days
- You see the credit [X] business days after processing

## 5. FAULTY OR COUNTERFEIT ITEMS

### Special Handling

If your item is:
- Defective or broken
- Counterfeit or replica
- Not as described
- Missing parts or components
- Damaged in shipping

### You get:

- Full refund (including delivery)
- No questions asked
- Return shipping paid by us
- Refund processed immediately upon receipt
- Option to keep the item (we may not want it back)

**No Return Window Applies:**

The [7]-day return window does NOT apply. You can claim a refund anytime if the item is genuinely defective or misdescribed.

## 6. EXCHANGES

Instead of a refund, you may request an exchange:

- Same item in different size/color
- Different item of equal value
- Different item of lower value (pay difference)
- Different item of higher value (pay difference)

Exchanges are processed at the same speed as refunds.

## 7. DISPUTES & COMPLAINTS

If you disagree with our refund decision:

- Contact our support team immediately
- Provide photos or evidence
- Explain why you believe you're entitled to a refund
- We will investigate within 7 days

If unresolved, use our Grievance Redressal Policy (response within 15 days).

## 8. YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES

When returning an item, you must:

- Return the item within the specified timeframe
- Pack it securely (damage in transit may reduce your refund)
- Include all original components and accessories
- Keep the return tracking number
- Not tamper with or further damage the item

If you don't follow these guidelines, we may reduce your refund or deny it.

## 9. SELLER'S RESPONSIBILITY

Sellers must:

- Accept all valid returns
- Not add restocking fees
- Cooperate with the return process
- Not cancel legitimate return requests
- Issue refunds promptly

If a seller refuses a valid return, we will:
- Force the refund
- Fine the seller
- Issue a strike against their account
- Suspend them if pattern continues

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

This policy complies with Nepal's E-Commerce Act 2081 and Consumer Protection Act 2075. Disputes are governed by Nepali law and courts of [DISTRICT].
