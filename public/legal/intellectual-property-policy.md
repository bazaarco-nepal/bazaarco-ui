# BazaarCo Intellectual Property Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

This policy protects brand owners, creators, and BazaarCo from counterfeit products and copyright or trademark misuse.

## 1. Counterfeit & Fake Brands

- Selling counterfeit, replica, or fake-branded goods is strictly prohibited.
- Sellers must hold the rights or authorization to sell any branded product they list.

## 2. Copyright in Listings & Content

- Images, videos, descriptions, and music used in a listing must be owned by the seller or used with permission.
- Using another party's photos or content without rights is not allowed.

## 3. Trademark Complaints

Brand and trademark owners can report listings that infringe their rights. Include:

- The trademark/brand and proof of ownership or authorization.
- Links to the infringing listing(s).
- A description of the infringement.

Send reports to support@bazaarconepal.com with the subject "IP COMPLAINT".

## 4. Takedown Process

1. We review the complaint and the reported listing.
2. If infringement is likely, we remove or disable the listing.
3. The seller is notified and may respond with evidence of rights.
4. We restore or keep the listing down based on the evidence.

## 5. Repeat Violations

Sellers with repeated IP violations face payout holds, suspension, or permanent termination under the [Seller Policy](/legal/seller-policy).

## 6. Relationship to Other Policies

This policy works together with the [Prohibited & Restricted Products Policy](/legal/prohibited-products-policy), [Product Listing Rules](/legal/product-listing-rules), and [Community Guidelines](/legal/community-guidelines).
