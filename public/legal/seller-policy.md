# BazaarCo Seller Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

This is the public Seller Policy summarising the rules every BazaarCo seller agrees to. The full, binding terms are in the [Seller Agreement](/legal/seller-agreement), which sellers accept during onboarding.

## 1. Seller Eligibility

Registered businesses, shops, and individual sellers may apply. Sellers must be able to fulfil orders reliably and comply with Nepali law.

## 2. Seller Documents Required

Under the E-Commerce Act 2081, before listing, sellers must provide:

- Full legal/business name and address
- Phone number and email
- PAN/VAT number
- Business registration evidence, where available or required
- Return, refund, and warranty details for their products
- A grievance/contact person
- Bank / eSewa / Khalti payout details

## 3. Listing Responsibility

Sellers are responsible for accurate listings, including title, price, images, stock, category, warranty, and all product details required under our [Product Listing Rules](/legal/product-listing-rules).

## 4. Order Fulfilment

Sellers must prepare and dispatch orders on time and keep stock information accurate. Repeated late or failed fulfilment may lead to penalties.

## 5. Returns & Refunds

Sellers must accept valid returns and refunds in line with the [Return & Refund Policy](/legal/return-and-refund-policy) and Nepal's Consumer Protection Act 2075.

## 6. Prohibited Conduct

- Fake, misleading, or counterfeit listings
- Fake reviews or self-reviews (see [Reviews & Community Guidelines](/legal/reviews-and-guidelines))
- Selling prohibited or restricted items (see [Prohibited Products Policy](/legal/prohibited-products-policy))
- Contacting buyers to settle disputes outside BazaarCo's process

## 7. Penalties

Violations may result in product removal, payout hold, account suspension, or termination, depending on severity and history.

## 8. BazaarCo's Rights

BazaarCo may inspect, edit, pause, or remove any seller or product to protect buyers and platform integrity.
