# BazaarCo Seller Payout & Settlement Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

This policy explains how and when sellers are paid for orders sold through BazaarCo, and what is deducted before payout.

## 1. Payout Cycle

Seller payouts are processed on a regular cycle (weekly or fortnightly). Each payout covers eligible orders that have cleared the settlement conditions below.

## 2. Payout Trigger

An order becomes eligible for payout after:

- Delivery is completed, **and**
- The return/dispute window for that order has cleared without an open claim.

## 3. Hold Period

Funds for an order may be held while a return, refund, or grievance is open. Once resolved, eligible amounts are released in the next payout cycle.

## 4. Deductions

The following are deducted before settlement, where applicable:

| Deduction             | Notes                                                           |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Category commission   | Per the [Commission Information](/legal/commission-information) |
| Delivery fee          | Where borne by the seller                                       |
| Pickup fee            | Where BazaarCo picks up from the seller                         |
| Refunds / chargebacks | For returned or refunded orders                                 |
| Payment gateway fee   | Where applicable                                                |
| TDS / VAT             | Where required by Nepali tax law                                |

## 5. Settlement Statement

Each payout is accompanied by a settlement statement in the seller dashboard, itemising orders, commission, deductions, and the net amount paid.

## 6. Tax & Records

Sellers are responsible for their own PAN/VAT compliance and invoicing. Under the E-Commerce Act 2081, BazaarCo maintains transaction records for the period prescribed by tax law.

## 7. Failed Payouts

Payouts may fail or be delayed due to incorrect bank/wallet details or a pending compliance/KYC issue. Update your payout details in the seller dashboard to resolve this.
