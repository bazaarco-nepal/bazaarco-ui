# BazaarCo Seller Delivery & Pickup Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

This policy covers how sellers prepare orders and how BazaarCo collects them for delivery. For buyer-facing delivery timelines and charges, see the [Shipping & Delivery Policy](/legal/shipping-and-delivery-policy).

## 1. Order Preparation

- Sellers must prepare and pack each order accurately and on time.
- Items must be securely packaged to prevent transit damage.
- The correct items, quantity, and any included accessories or warranty cards must be packed.

## 2. Pickup Model

| Item              | Detail                                               |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Pickup fee        | Rs 60 per BazaarCo pickup from the seller            |
| Additional pickup | Rs 10 after 3 orders, where applicable               |
| Pickup window     | Per the schedule shown in the seller dashboard       |
| Partner delivery  | PickNDrop or another partner may collect and deliver |

Fees are deducted at settlement per the [Seller Payout & Settlement Policy](/legal/seller-payout-and-settlement-policy).

## 3. Service Area

Delivery initially covers the Kathmandu Valley. Coverage and pickup zones are shown during onboarding and may expand over time.

## 4. Cutoff Times

To meet same-day or next-day delivery, orders must be ready by the cutoff time shown in the dashboard (for example, before 10 AM for same-day, where offered).

## 5. Failed or Delayed Pickup

- If a seller is unavailable or the order is not ready at pickup, the pickup may be rescheduled and repeat failures may incur charges.
- Delays caused by weather, strikes, or force majeure are handled case by case.

## 6. Delivery Liability

Under the E-Commerce Act 2081, where there is a contract to deliver goods to a designated person, place, or time, delivery liability lies with the platform even when a delivery partner is used. BazaarCo coordinates delivery and related complaints; sellers remain responsible for correct, timely, well-packaged orders.
