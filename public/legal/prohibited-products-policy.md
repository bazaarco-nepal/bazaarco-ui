# BazaarCo Prohibited & Restricted Products Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## OVERVIEW

To protect our customers and comply with Nepali law, BazaarCo prohibits certain products entirely and restricts others to authorized sellers with valid licenses.

Sellers who list prohibited items will have their listings removed and may face account suspension or permanent termination.

## 1. ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED PRODUCTS

The following items **CANNOT** be sold on BazaarCo under any circumstances. Listing these items will result in immediate removal, strikes against your account, and potential legal action.

### 1.1 Counterfeit, Replica & Stolen Goods
- Counterfeit branded items (fake designer goods, fake electronics)
- Replica products (watches, handbags, shoes, apparel)
- Stolen goods
- Gray market or diverted goods without proper authorization

### 1.2 Illegally Imported & Smuggled Goods
- Goods imported without proper customs documentation
- Smuggled items
- Goods with duty unpaid
- Goods imported from sanctioned countries
- Undocumented imports

### 1.3 Weapons & Explosives
- Firearms, pistols, revolvers
- Ammunition
- Grenades, bombs, explosive devices
- Explosives and blasting agents
- Gun parts and components
- Silencers or weapon attachments

### 1.4 Narcotics & Controlled Substances
- Marijuana, cannabis, hashish
- Cocaine, heroin, opium, morphine
- LSD, MDMA, ecstasy
- Methamphetamine
- Synthetic drugs, legal highs
- Drug paraphernalia (pipes, rolling papers if marketed for drugs)

### 1.5 Unapproved & Counterfeit Medicines
- Fake or counterfeit medicines
- Medicines without proper registration from government
- Narcotics without prescription
- Pharmaceutical samples
- Prescription drugs without valid prescription requirement
- Unregistered herbal or Ayurvedic medicines

### 1.6 Wildlife & Protected Species
- Live wildlife (endangered animals)
- Animal skins, furs, pelts (from endangered species)
- Ivory and ivory products
- Rhino horn or rhino products
- Tiger/leopard products
- Bird feathers and plumes
- Protected plant species

### 1.7 Hazardous & Recalled Products
- Items on government recall lists
- Products banned in Nepal
- Asbestos and asbestos products
- Lead-based paints
- Products with known safety defects
- Hazardous chemicals (without proper licensing)

### 1.8 Intellectual Property Infringement
- Products infringing patents
- Software or media infringing copyrights
- Counterfeit software or licenses
- Unauthorized bootleg copies
- Unlicensed digital content

### 1.9 Illegal Services or Digital Content
- Illegal services
- Stolen digital content
- Pirated movies, music, software
- Hacked accounts or credentials
- Fake diplomas or certificates
- Leaked personal information

### 1.10 Child Safety Items
- Any product intended to harm or exploit children
- Products marketed for child abuse
- Unsafe toys that violate safety standards

### 1.11 Any Item Prohibited Under Nepali Law
Any item banned or prohibited under:
- E-Commerce Act 2081
- Consumer Protection Act 2075
- Penal Code 2074
- Any other Nepali federal or provincial law

Sellers are responsible for knowing the law.

## 2. RESTRICTED PRODUCTS (Requires License or Documentation)

The following items **CAN** be sold ONLY if you have valid documentation and proper authorization. Listing without authorization will result in removal and strikes.

### 2.1 Alcohol & Liquor
**Requirements:**
- Valid liquor license from local authorities
- Age verification system on listing
- Marked "Age 18+ Only"
- Cannot be delivered to minors
- Complies with local alcohol regulations

### 2.2 Tobacco & Smoking Products
**Requirements:**
- Valid tobacco business license
- Age verification system
- Marked "Age 18+ Only"
- Warning labels displayed
- Cannot be delivered to minors

### 2.3 Certain Electronics
**Requirements:**
- Valid dealer/distributor license
- For items like radios, transmitters
- Import permits (if applicable)
- Government registration

### 2.4 Regulated Health Products
**Requirements:**
- Valid pharmacy or health product license
- For items like vitamins, minerals, health supplements
- Registration with health department
- Cannot make medical claims

### 2.5 Pesticides & Chemicals
**Requirements:**
- Valid pesticide/chemical dealer license
- Storage and handling certification
- Cannot be delivered to certain areas

### 2.6 Prescription Medications
**Requirements:**
- Licensed pharmacy only
- Prescription verification system
- Doctor's prescription required
- Licensed pharmacist verification

## 3. ENFORCEMENT & CONSEQUENCES

### Our Enforcement Process

1. **Detection:** Automated system flags prohibited items, user reports violations, BazaarCo manual review
2. **Action:** Item removed immediately, seller notified, strike issued
3. **Escalation:** Based on number of violations

### Escalation

- **First violation:** Warning + education
- **Second violation:** Account suspension (7 days)
- **Third violation:** Permanent account termination
- **Serious violations:** Immediate permanent removal + legal referral

### Serious Violations

Counterfeits, drugs, weapons, or endangered species:
- Immediate account termination
- Reported to law enforcement
- Cooperation with investigations
- Forfeiture of settlements (if applicable)

### Criminal Penalties

Under E-Commerce Act 2081 §23:
- Selling prohibited items: Fine NPR 50,000–500,000 + imprisonment
- Counterfeit goods: Criminal charges
- Drugs: Criminal charges (Section on narcotic drugs)
- Endangered species: Criminal charges

- You are responsible for legality of your products
- You hold all necessary licenses and certifications
- You will defend BazaarCo in any legal action arising from your products
- You will pay all fines and legal costs
