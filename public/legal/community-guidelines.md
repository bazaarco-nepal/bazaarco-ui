# BazaarCo Community Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

BazaarCo is a video-first marketplace where sellers upload product videos and buyers share reviews. These guidelines keep that content honest, safe, and lawful. They apply alongside the [Reviews & Community Guidelines](/legal/reviews-and-guidelines) for ratings and feedback.

## 1. Seller Videos & Content

- Videos must show the **actual product** being sold.
- No misleading edits, fake demonstrations, or exaggerated claims.
- Content must accurately represent condition, size, and features.

## 2. Prohibited Content

| Not allowed        | Examples                                        |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Misleading content | Fake demos, false claims, bait listings         |
| Offensive content  | Hate speech, harassment, explicit material      |
| Copyright content  | Media you do not own or have rights to          |
| Unlicensed music   | Tracks used without rights                      |
| Illegal content    | Anything promoting prohibited goods or activity |

## 3. Music & Third-Party Media

Sellers are responsible for ensuring they have the rights to any music, images, or footage used in their content. See the [Intellectual Property Policy](/legal/intellectual-property-policy).

## 4. Respectful Conduct

Buyers and sellers must communicate respectfully. Harassment, threats, abusive language, and spam are not tolerated in messages, reviews, or content.

## 5. Reporting Violations

If you see content that breaks these guidelines, report it via the listing/content menu or email support@bazaarconepal.com with details.

## 6. BazaarCo's Rights

BazaarCo may remove content, hide listings, or suspend accounts that violate these guidelines. Repeat or serious violations may lead to permanent removal.
